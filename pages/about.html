About ArtCycle

Who We Are

ArtCycle is a student-led movement to recycle waste at AISVN. We aim to provide sustainable recycled art materials for AISVN Art and Design department, and create a greener community at AISVN.

SDG Goal 12

Sustainable Development Goal 12 is about Responsible Consumption and Production. Think about your plastic cup or snacks wrapper. Goal 12 is about making sure we don't use too much or throw things away too quickly. We want to recycle, reuse, and be smart about how we handle waste. Let's take care of our school and meet SDG Goal 12 by recycling at AISVN!

Solid Waste Issue

Solid Waste in Vietnam

Solid waste is a big issue in Vietnam, taking its place within the top 20 countries with the largest amount of waste globally. A lot of these waste aren't recycled and sorted properly. These wastes are mostly dumped into landfills where they continue polluting the environment.


Solid Waste Globally

Globally, the solid waste is also big challenge. Our world has 8 billion people living in it. With growing populations, we will consume more resources which in turn produces more wastes. Efforts to recycle and reduce waste have taken place to address this problem but we need to get everyone to participate!
