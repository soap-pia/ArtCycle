Recycling Services at AISVN

Recycling Method

Location: Room 322, third floor art storage
You can recycle materials and waste at the art storage room. Come with your clean materials and sort your waste into the correct bin!

What should you recycle? We're looking for...

Paper

You can recycle all kinds of paper: newspaper, scrap paper, and more.

Plastic

From wrappers and bags, plastic is everywhere. Recycle for a greener environment!

Cardboard

Have old cardboard packaging or projects laying around? Recycle it!

Cloth

Don't throw away your old clothes. Recycle it for the environment!
